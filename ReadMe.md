# Altis Cards

## About

Altis Cards is a modern web application dedicated to providing customers with premium-quality custom cards featuring elegant designs, durable construction, and exceptional craftsmanship. Our mission is to combine style, quality, and personalization into a seamless online shopping experience.

Customers can browse a curated collection of professionally designed cards and choose the material that best fits their needs. Every design is available in multiple finishes, including traditional paper, paper/plastic composite, and 100% premium plastic, allowing users to select the perfect balance of appearance, durability, and feel.

Whether you're looking for business cards, membership cards, loyalty cards, or personalized custom designs, Altis Cards makes ordering simple, secure, and convenient.

---

## Features

* Google Sign-In for quick and secure account access
*  Integrated online storefront for browsing and purchasing products
*  Amazon integration for selected products and fulfillment
*  Modern, responsive user interface
*  Multiple material options:

  * Premium Paper
  * Paper/Plastic Composite
  * 100% Plastic
  *  Mobile-friendly design
  *  Fast and intuitive shopping experience
